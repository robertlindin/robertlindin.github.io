Optimization

Some of the things we worked with in this project:

  • Better packaged stories
  • Increased Editor control of pace and dynamics in the news flow
  • Higher tempo in the news flow
  • Increased information density
  • More usable and dynamic use of breaking events like for example: “Right now” or “Live”
  • Increased focused on a compact “Latest news” section
  • Rebuilt right column with increased tempo and dynamics
  • Mobile- and touch friendly menus
  • Slow but high quality news repositioned to swipe areas in the bottom of the page
  • Labeling of sections and navigation to sections in the main news flow, increased of menu navigation
  • Rebuilt article with increased options around hierarchy and associated media
  • News related to the Story of the article placed in a swipe box in the bottom of the article
  • Touch-friendly icons and buttons

…and obviously in a project of this size a lot more unmentioned things.
